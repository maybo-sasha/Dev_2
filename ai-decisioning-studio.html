AI Decisioning Studio

Making advanced AI decisioning something marketers actually understand, trust and activate.

AI Decisioning Studio is a centralized dashboard I designed at Optimove to move marketers away from rigid manual rules and toward predictive intelligence. The machine learning models were powerful, but the tech was hidden behind dense, black-box screens. Marketers simply did not trust what they could not see.

The challenge

Powerful AI, zero adoption

The optimization models worked perfectly in production, but user adoption was near zero. My mandate was to close the gap between engineering capability and user trust.

01

The black-box barrier

Marketers could not visualize what the AI was doing or why. Fearing a loss of control or double-messaging their audience, they left the automation off and kept running campaigns by hand.

02

Fragmented optimization

Maximizing a campaign meant managing disconnected secondary parameters across multiple platform tools, with no unified sense of how much lift was being left on the table.

03

Human-in-the-loop balance

To comfortably hand control to an autonomous model, users needed clear system visibility, transparent backend staging, and effortless manual override guardrails.

My role

Owning the experience end to end

As the Senior Product Designer, I led the product experience end to end: mapping cross-functional data states with engineering, defining the holistic information architecture, and crafting the interaction mechanics for the automated activation flow.

Most of the hard work was conceptual: turning raw model output into something a marketer could reason about. That started with the Optimization Score and carried through the agent system, the flows, the final UI and the patterns I fed back into the design system for the rest of the suite to reuse.

Strategy lobby

A command center, not a folder

The Strategy Lobby is the stable information-architecture roof for a marketer's entire customer lifecycle. Instead of treating campaigns like flat, unorganized lists, the interface clusters them under high-level business strategies.

A master card architecture surfaces the overall Optimization Score and projected financial headroom at a glance, immediately answering "where is my attention worth spending" before asking the user to configure a single setting.

Inner strategy page

Taming the densest interface in the product

Opening a strategy reveals its deep performance metrics over time. The main UX challenge here was managing severe screen data density. I implemented a strict master-detail layout that anchors the viewport on three core elements.

Score

The Optimization Score

A high-contrast circular progress ring shows exactly how much available lift a strategy has safely unlocked. For complete transparency, the score is broken down by fully, partially and un-optimized workflows.

Signal

Live AI signals

Every campaign row carries a dynamic percentage tracking remaining uplift potential. This instantly turns a standard table into a prioritized, scannable checklist of where manual intervention is needed most.

Suggestion

Actionable suggestions

Sitting right beside each live signal is a plain-language system recommendation, for example add Send Time Optimization to boost open rates. Insight and action are never separated, and the setup modal is always one click away.

The decisioning agents

Designing an autonomous system

I designed our four primary AI decisioning engines as a cohesive visual and typographic family, using a standard "an AI agent that..." description pattern. When a user meets a new model, they instantly understand its functional boundaries and core business value.

SOJ

Journey Decisioning

An AI agent that dynamically prioritizes overlapping campaigns based on predicted future value. It replaces manual priority rules, acting as a smart traffic controller when a customer qualifies for multiple workflows on the same day.

SOC

Offer Decisioning

An AI agent that automatically tests and delivers the optimal offer variation for each customer. It moves past static, winner-takes-all A/B testing by isolating incentives to protect business margins.

STO

Send Time Optimization

An AI agent that predicts the ideal delivery window so messages arrive when customers are most active. It analyzes individual engagement histories to maximize opens and clicks.

CVO

Content Decisioning

An AI agent that optimizes content variations in real time to deliver the ideal message. It eliminates creative chaos by continuously selecting the best layout and tone combinations for every recipient.

The activation flow

Earning trust at the moment of commitment

The climax of the experience is the modal activation flow. To bridge the trust gap, the modal walks users through a transparent, two-stage system handover.

Stage 1

The transparent staging list

Before turning an agent on, the UI abandons vague "Apply" jargon and spells out the exact destructive and constructive backend steps the AI is about to execute, for example pause active campaign, duplicate target group, clone baseline action. Nothing is a surprise, which builds real operational trust.

Stage 2

The single-line transition

On "Apply," the interface renders an inline, half-opacity ghosted-green "Calculating" state instead of a sentence-breaking spinner, and the UI stays fully interactive. Once processing completes, the text scales up to full opacity with a smooth particle pop that celebrates the successful automation.
