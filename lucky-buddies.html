Lucky Buddies

Scroll
RoleHead of Product Design
CompanyLucky Buddies
DisciplineGame UI · Branding · Motion · Specs
PlatformiOS · Android · Mobile-first

Lucky Buddies is a mobile game where casual play meets RPG strategy — a slot-driven core loop that pulls players deeper into the world with every spin. Collect Buddies, level them up, build your team, and face off against the evil Pi-Rats in escalating battles. Each session promises new heroes, fresh loot and the next dramatic showdown.

As Head of Product Design, my remit ran from the smallest icon to the wider visual system: a graphical language consistent across slots, popups, team views and battle scenes, supported by the specs and wireframes that kept art, engineering and live-ops aligned. I owned the palette, the motion direction and the iconic branding — driving the studio toward an interface that’s punchy, expressive and unmistakably Lucky Buddies.

01.

Main Game

The slot reel is more than a one-armed bandit — it’s the engine of progression. Symbols translate into currency, attacks and Buddy unlocks; bright icon design, tactile spin feedback and a clear progression bar make every pull rewarding. Players don’t just spin to win — they spin to advance.

02.

Monetization Section

Offers live where the action is, not buried in a store. A persistent left rail surfaces time-limited deals, daily refresh packs and one-tap upgrade bundles directly in the player’s eyeline. It’s designed to feel like opportunity, not interruption — players who want an edge get one without ever leaving the spin.

03.

Upgrade from the Main Game

Upgrading shouldn’t break flow. Tap the active Buddy from inside the main game and the upgrade sheet unfolds in place — stats, level, abilities and costs all visible without a screen change. Players invest, return to the reels and feel the impact on the very next spin.

04.

Team Page & Buddy Page

The roster is the meta layer of the game. The Team page presents every Buddy by rarity — common, legendary, epic — both unlocked heroes and locked teasers for what’s coming next. Tap any Buddy for a dedicated page: stats, items, level progression and a clear path to the next upgrade. The full collection, always two taps from the slot.

05.

Buddies Islands

Every Buddy leader anchors their own island. Climb the levels and the world expands — new islands surface, new heroes appear, and the map keeps growing. It’s progression you can see geographically: a living world that gives players a reason to keep spinning.
